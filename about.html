HI, I'm Christina.

As a child I used to gaze across Lake Michigan curious about the world, the different people and cultures. As an adult, I traveled the world. After marrying my husband, we spent the past 11 years living traveling the world living all over the U.S. and Asia - 8 places in 11 years. The global perspective and orientation from years of travel has only enhanced my love for design and U.X. while fostering a curious open growth mindset.

“I’m just a girl chasing her dream and having an amazing adventure.”

- Madaline Stuart

Giving back to others

Being a part of and giving back to my community has always given me great satisfaction as well as a sense of purpose. For me, a part of giving back has always included working with children. I truly believe that today’s youth will make a stronger tomorrow. This is the reason that I am involved in projects such as “Project Cornerstone” where I children learn about core values such as responsibility, courage, honor, patience and integrity. Along the way, I’ve realized it’s not me challenging children, instead it is them challenging me. From their ideas, questions, creativity and fresh perspective children are our greatest hope for the future.
